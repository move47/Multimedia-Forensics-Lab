Approach

  • The documents/sentences are first pre-processed to remove special characters and stop words from the text.
  • The processed text is then represented using pre-trained text2vec state-of-art deep learning models.
  • The vector representation of the texts is then used to calculate Cosine Similarity and Euclidean Distance.

Conclusion

This work is some short of survey covering several pre-trained models for the quantification of similarity between documents using Cosine Similarity and Euclidean distance as two metrics.
